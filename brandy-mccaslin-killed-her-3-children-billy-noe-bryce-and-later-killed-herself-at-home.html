Brandy McCaslin has been identified as the mother suspected of murdering her three children before killing herself in Verdigris, Oklahoma.

The Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation, as well as those who knew her on social media, published McCaslin’s identify in a statement.

Billy Jacobson posted images of the children to his Facebook page, along with the message, “Please keep us in your prayers.” Brandy McCaslin committed murder-suicide with all three of her children. Billy, Noe, Bryce, and Brandy are no longer with us.”

Officers with the Verdigris Police Department “noticed fireworks erupting outside a home near East Dogwood Court and Cypress Street” on the afternoon of July 20, 2023. Officers proceeded to investigate and discovered an armed and barricaded lady inside the home,” according to an Oklahoma State Bureau of Investigation press release.

“Negotiations quickly began as they attempted to contact Brandy McCaslin, 39.” For the following three hours, there was a standoff. After receiving no response, officers entered the house and discovered McCaslin and her three children dead inside.”
