David Choi (born March 22, 1986 in Anaheim, California, United States) is a Los Angeles-based musician and YouTube video producer. Choi’s original music and pop covers have been featured on NBC, FOX, VH1, MTV, A&E, E!, Travel Channel, Style Network, PBS, Food Network, Disney, and in advertisements all over the world. Kellogg’s, Starburst, the American Cancer Society, General Electric, Samsung, J. C. Penney, and Asian online fashion and cosmetics store YesStyle are among his clients. Only You, Choi’s first album, was released in October 200On May 19, 2010, he published his second album, By My Side, and on October 25, 2011, he released his third album, Forever and Ever. David Choi’s YouTube Channel has 929,061 subscribers, 117,774,875 total video views, and over 13 million channel views as of December 3, 201He was the 15th most-subscribed artist on YouTube and the 62nd most-subscribed user overall as of November 201On Twitter, he has 120,385 followers as of March 7, 201Choi formerly worked as a composer and producer at Warner/Chappell Music, where he was hired after his participation in an ASCAP workshop. Over 2.8 million people have seen his funny song “YouTube, A Love Song.” The music videos for his songs “Won’t Even Start” and “That Girl,” which are available on both his and Wong Fu Productions’ YouTube accounts, have garnered over 5 million views. On his second channel, he also uploads non-music vlogs. Choi often appears in online videos with other YouTube celebrities, usually as a straight-faced comedic figure.
