Jerry Williams: the next big thing in 2016

September 09, 2024
blog

Last night at The Old Queen’s Head in Angel, Jerry Williams performed a private gig.  She is promoting her new single ‘Boy Oh Boy’ which she performed last night.

The Guardian have tipped her to be a big thing in 2016 and her music is said to be a retro take on early Lilly Allen-esque pop.

She clearly bases her music around personal experiences as she wrote a song called ‘cocktails with my cousin’ after having a night out with her cousin. This was one of the tracks she played for us last night.

Being a 19 year old girl some tracks are about boys like ‘tie kicker’ which is about boys who waste your time.

Jerry also played ‘Velcro’ which is a song she performs by herself which is based around the feeling of not being able to let someone go.
