Biography:

Rebecca Liddiard was born in 1991. She attended Ryerson University where she studied theater. At age 18, she relocated to Toronto. In 2013, she made her screen debut in an episode of the television series Murdoch Mysteries. Her next role was in an episode of Man Seeking Woman. Her first recurring role was as Hanna in the Canadian series Between. At the same time, she had her first starring role as Ella on the web series MsLabelled.

She next guest-starred in an episode of Saving Hope. In 2016, she starred as Adelaide Stratton in the TV show Houdini & Doyle, as well as starred in the TV movie For Love & Honor. In 2017, she portrayed Princess Margot in an episode of Reign. At the same time, she gained recognition for her role as Mary Whitney in the miniseries Alias Grace. She also joined the main cast of Slasher for its second season. As of 2017, she stars as Mary Shaw in Frankie Drake Mysteries.
