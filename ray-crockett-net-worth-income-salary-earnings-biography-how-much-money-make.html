“Ray” Crockett (born January 5, 1967) is a retired American football player who played with the New York Giants of the National Football League. He was a cornerback with the Denver Broncos for fourteen seasons and won two Super Bowl rings during his tenure with the franchise. Duncanville High School in Duncanville, Texas, was where he competed in high school football for four years. He was a member of the Baylor Bears’ football team from 1984 to 1988, and he was inducted into the Baylor Athletic Hall of Fame the following year. During the 2005 season of the ESPN reality programme Bound for Glory, he co-starred alongside Dick Butkus, in which they each took on the role of head football coach for a high school football team.
