What is Marin Ireland's Net Worth?

Marin Ireland is an American actress who has a net worth of $1 million. Marin Ireland was born in 1980. She trained at the Idyllwild Arts Foundation and graduated from The Hartt School at the University of Hartford. Ireland appeared in episodes of Law & Order: Criminal Intent in 2003 and 2006.
